Planned Giving at IIT Kanpur is more than a gift — it is a lasting commitment to knowledge, innovation, and impact. Through thoughtful planning today, you can help build tomorrow’s breakthroughs, empower generations of students, and advance the institute’s mission for decades to come. Whether through bequests, endowments, or other legacy gifts, your vision will be forever woven into the fabric of IIT Kanpur’s excellence.

Every planned gift, regardless of its size, carries the power to inspire. It ensures that IIT Kanpur continues to nurture leaders, fuel path-breaking research, and serve as a beacon of progress for India and the world.
